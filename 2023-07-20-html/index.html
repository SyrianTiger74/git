how to make a swiss roll easy way

ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 400g condensed milk
  • 100g cacao
  • 400g cookies

instruction step by step

  1. break the cookies in a pot
  2. put another pot on medium heat with the butter insider
  3. when the butter start to melt pour the condensed milk while stearing
  4. when it come together pour the cacao still stearing
  5. boil till it comes all together
  6. pour the mixture on the cookies and stear till all cookies covered with the mixture
  7. leave it a bit to rest then make the form you want , leave till come to room tempreture
  8. put in the fridge for 6-8 hours better ovr noght
